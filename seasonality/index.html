Seasonality Analysis

Methodology

  • Yahoo finance daily OHLC tuples since year 2000 (if available) are the source data
  • The close of the first trading day of the respective year is the percentage baseline
  • On a yearly timeframe, every day's close is normalized towards this baseline
  • On a monthly basis, data is aggregated by linearly counting trading days to compensate for weekday fluctuation.
  • Only the first 19 trading days are used to ensure equal data distribution - this can be potentially optimized by averaging the values found in the end of the month. But otherwise there are potential gaps/jumps because only one out of 10 years in the sequence had a 22 trading day or similiar.
  • The values in the daily bins are averaged to derive the graphs for the respective time frames.
  • All data was obtained and diagrams were created on 2021-08-01.

All graphs are interactive, i.e. lines can be shown/hidden by clicking them in the legend on the side.
